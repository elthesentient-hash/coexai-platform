## When to Choose Humans

**Choose humans when:**
- Complex negotiation required
- High-stakes creative decisions
- Building deep relationships
- First-of-its-kind strategy
- Emotional intelligence critical

**Choose AI when:**
- High volume, repetitive tasks
- 24/7 coverage needed
- Speed is critical
- Consistency matters
- Cost efficiency important
