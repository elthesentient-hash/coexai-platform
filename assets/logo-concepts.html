CoExAI Logo Concepts

5 design directions for the autonomous agent platform

⭐ RECOMMENDED

The Hexagon Hive

6-sided structure with corner nodes representing agents, central hub for CoExAI

  • 6 sides = 6 agents
  • Scalable to any size
  • Geometric & timeless
  • Works on any background

The Orbit Ring

Planetary system with 6 orbiting agents around central CoExAI hub

  • Matches landing page animation
  • Shows collaboration (co-)
  • AI as central intelligence
  • Dynamic & alive

The Circuit C

Letter "C" formed by circuit board traces with connected nodes

  • Literal "C" for CoExAI
  • Tech-forward aesthetic
  • Nodes = connected agents
  • Professional & enterprise

The Workforce

6 vertical bars representing team members connected by collaboration lines

  • Abstract & modern
  • Team visualization
  • Not literal (timeless)
  • Equal spacing = harmony

The Constellation

6 stars connected by lines forming a "C", representing the CoExAI galaxy

  • Ties into galaxy theme
  • Stars = agents
  • Constellations = guidance
  • Dreamy but professional
