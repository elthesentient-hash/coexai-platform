Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing

How does the 30-day money-back guarantee work?

If you're not completely satisfied with your AI agent within the first 30 days, simply contact our support team for a full refund. No questions asked, no hoops to jump through. We want you to be 100% confident in your investment.

Can I switch between agents or upgrade to the bundle?

Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch between individual agents at any time. If you start with one agent and want to add more, we'll prorate any differences. Upgrading to the bundle gives you the best value.

What's included in each agent's monthly service?

Each agent includes unlimited tasks within their specialty, 24/7 availability, direct communication via Slack, and weekly progress reports. Specific outputs vary by agent: Maya delivers unlimited content, Jordan provides strategy and optimization, Sam offers 40 hours of development work, and Taylor handles unlimited operational tasks.

Is there a setup fee or long-term contract?

No setup fees, no long-term contracts. All plans are month-to-month (or annual with 20% savings). You can cancel anytime with 7 days notice before your next billing cycle. We believe in earning your business every month.

How do the agents communicate with my team?

Each agent integrates directly into your existing workflow via Slack, email, or your preferred project management tool. They respond to requests, provide updates, and collaborate with your team just like a human employee would. Jordan and Sam also include weekly strategy/standup calls.

What happens if I need more than one agent's specialty?

You can hire multiple individual agents or choose the Complete AI Team bundle at $1,995/month (saves you $599/month). The bundle also includes priority support and custom integrations so your agents work together seamlessly as a unified team.
