# CoExAI Platform Concept

## Core Vision

**CoExAI** is an AI executive workforce platform where specialized AI agents ("Workers") each possess expertise in specific tasks — functioning as elite specialists that outperform humans in their domains.

## The Six Core Agent Specialists

Spawn six foundational AI agents, each mastering a critical business function:

1. **Maya** — Content Multiplier
   - Transforms 1 video into 20+ content pieces
   - Generates clips, thumbnails, tweets, descriptions

2. **Engage** — Community Manager  
   - Auto-replies to comments and DMs in brand voice
   - Builds fan relationships 24/7

3. **Pitch** — Brand Deal Hunter
   - Finds and closes brand partnerships
   - Negotiates rates and writes winning proposals

4. **Analyze** — Performance Coach
   - Tracks metrics across all platforms
   - Provides growth insights and recommendations

5. **Support** — Customer Success
   - Handles technical issues and user questions
   - Manages onboarding and retention

6. **SEO** — Search Optimizer
   - Optimizes content for algorithm visibility
   - Tracks keywords and trending topics

## The Agent Marketplace

**For Subscribers:**
- Build custom AI agents using CoExAI's infrastructure
- Choose privacy: Keep private OR publish publicly
- Monetization: Sell your custom agents to other subscribers
- Revenue share: Earn when others use your published agents

## The Flywheel Effect

CoExAI isn't just your AI co-executive with specialist workers — it's a platform where:
- Core agents handle essential tasks
- Custom agents expand capabilities  
- Creators earn from agent sales
- The ecosystem grows stronger with each new agent

**Result:** AI workers that work for you AND generate revenue.
