## FAQ

### Q: How quickly can I get started?
**A:** You can deploy any agent in under 5 minutes. Most users see their first results within 24-48 hours.

### Q: Do I need technical skills to use these agents?
**A:** Not at all. Each agent has a simple dashboard where you set preferences and review outputs. We handle all the technical complexity.

### Q: Can I customize the agents to match my brand/voice?
**A:** Absolutely. All agents learn your voice, style, and preferences. The more you use them, the better they get at sounding like you.

### Q: What happens to my data? Is it secure?
**A:** Your data is encrypted and never shared. Maya processes content locally on your machine. Enterprise plans include SOC 2 compliance.

### Q: Can I cancel anytime?
**A:** Yes, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with no penalties.

### Q: Is there a free trial?
**A:** Yes, every agent includes a 7-day free trial so you can test before committing.

### Q: What if I'm not satisfied?
**A:** We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not happy, we'll refund 100% of your payment.

### Q: Can I use multiple agents together?
**A:** Yes, and we offer bundle discounts. Many customers use all four agents as their complete AI workforce.

### Q: How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?
**A:** CoExAI agents are specialized and autonomous. They don't just generate content—they execute complete workflows, integrate with platforms, and deliver measurable business results.

### Q: Do you offer enterprise plans?
**A:** Yes, we offer custom enterprise plans with dedicated support, custom integrations, and volume pricing. Contact us for details.

### Q: What platforms do the agents integrate with?
**A:** Jordan integrates with LinkedIn. Maya works with YouTube, podcasts, and all social platforms. Sam connects with Google Business Profile. Taylor integrates with most CRMs, databases, and storage systems.

### Q: How accurate is Taylor's document processing?
**A:** Taylor achieves 99.7% accuracy on structured documents. For complex or handwritten documents, accuracy may vary, and we provide confidence scores for each extraction.

### Q: Can I white-label these agents for my agency?
**A:** Yes, our Agency plan allows you to resell CoExAI agents under your own brand.

### Q: What kind of support do you offer?
**A:** All plans include email support. Professional plans include priority support. Enterprise plans include dedicated account managers and Slack support.

### Q: How do I train Jordan to write in my voice?
**A:** Jordan analyzes your existing content (posts, articles, emails) to learn your style. You can also provide writing samples and give feedback on outputs to refine the match.

### Q: Can Maya handle video content?
**A:** Yes, Maya can process videos from YouTube, podcasts, or direct uploads. She extracts key segments, generates clips, and creates multi-format content.

### Q: How does Sam improve my Google ranking?
**A:** Sam optimizes your Google Business Profile, manages reviews, builds local citations, monitors competitors, and implements proven local SEO strategies.

### Q: Is there a limit to how many documents Taylor can process?
**A:** Processing limits vary by plan. Starter plans include 1,000 documents/month. Professional plans include 10,000. Enterprise plans have custom limits.

### Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
**A:** Yes, you can change plans at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.
