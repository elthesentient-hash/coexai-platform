## APPENDICES

### Appendix A: One-Liner Power Prompts
Key prompts for rapid assessment:
1. "Walk me through your reasoning step-by-step"
2. "What's the strongest argument against your conclusion?"
3. "If this is true, what else must be true?"
4. "What's the general principle behind this specific case?"
5. "Teach me this like I'm smart but know nothing"
6. "What am I missing when I look at this?"
7. "What patterns do you see that aren't obvious?"
8. "What could go wrong and how do we prepare?"
9. "What's the opposite of the obvious solution?"
10. "Roast my work. Be ruthless but constructive."
11. "What's the hardest truth about [skill] that most people avoid?"
12. "What question should I be asking that I'm not asking?"
